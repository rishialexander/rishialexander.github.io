Ripe for growth

Every day the legal marijuana industry gets bigger and bigger with more growers, retailers, and consumers. But the industry’s rapid growth has made it difficult for growers and retailers to remain competitive and for consumers to find the products they love.

Many products, many problems

It’s an exciting time to be in the marijuana industry – there are a lot of products that have sprouted overnight to fill the growing needs of the industry. We looked to see where we could make the most impact.

Wide
eyed consumers

Growers, retailers, and producers range from multi-million dollar operations to small quality-driven shops. Each player faces their own problems, but the industry will only evolve as fast as its consumers learn about the product and how it fits into their lifestyle.

Growing potential

Consumers rarely see the rich story, culture, and care behind the products they buy. We believe that by connecting consumers to the story behind products they can connect more intimately with the products they love.

A brighter future

Hound gives marijuana users the tools to become sophisticated and discerning consumers by giving them information to choose the marijuana that fits into their lifestyle. By connecting the grower, retailer, and consumer Hound empowers the marijuana industry towards a more happy and healthy future.
