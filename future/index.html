the app
inspiration

A fun, simple way to learn about how marijuana fits into your lifestyle.

Hound puts a budtender in your pocket and keeps you connected with your favorite stores and growers so that you always find great products.

Explore everything that marijuana has to offer.

Now that marijuana is more accessible than ever, you have thousands of choices whenever you want to try something new. The Budtender can help you find the right one for you, whatever you’re feeling.

Figure out what works for you, and what doesn't.

Log all the products that you use, keep track of your favorites and share feedback directly with the growers. Hound uses your feedback to give you better recommendations in the future.

Connect directly with growers for the first time.

Go beyond the packaging and learn about the growers who put their passion into growing. Discover their philosophies, see photos of their growing methods and give them direct feedback to make their next batch perfect.

Become a tastemaker and get exclusive access to the latest products.

If you’re a frequent flyer, your local retailer can reach out to you and give you exclusive access to the latest products. Let them know what you think—if you like it, you might see it in stock soon.
